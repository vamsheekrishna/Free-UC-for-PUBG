Privacy Policy

Free Royalpass & UC (“Service”, “Free Royalpass & UC”, "we," "us," or "our") collects information from you in order to provide better service and better user experience. Your privacy is a top priority to us, and this Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

What information we use and how we use it:

  • Read phone status and identity
    This permission allows Free Royalpass & UC to recognize an incoming/outgoing call and to switch between the User System interface and call interface or announces/shows the caller name while someone is calling, you will identify it without looking to your smartphone.
  • Read your text messjjages (SMS or MMS)

    We do not save or upload your messages. We use this permission to read your text messages when you search messages in the search bar or announce the sender's name while someone is send you a message, you will identify it without looking to your smartphone.

  • Take pictures and videos

    This permission allows Free Royalpass & UC to use your device’s camera to take photos/videos and turn ON/OFF Camera Flash. We do not save or upload your photos/videos.

  • Approximate location (network-based)

    This permission allows Free Royalpass & UC to identify and display your location on maps or apps installed by anonymous surrounding users and to recommend popular apps based on users’ locations. This information is untraceable.

  • Read your contacts

    Free Royalpass & UC does not save or upload your contacts. Permission to access contact information is used when you search contacts in Free Royalpass & UC search bar.

  • Read calendar events plus confidential information

    This information is used only in the Free Royalpass & UC to remind you of your latest calendar events. We do not save or upload any of your calendar information.

  • Modify or delete the contents of your USB storage

    This permission allows Free Royalpass & UC to delete app data(image, text, audio, etc..) stored on their SD card.

  • Read the contents of your USB storage

    This permission allows Free Royalpass & UC to view app data(image, text, audio, etc..) stored on their SD card.

  • Set an alarm

    This permission allows users to access the device's alarm through Free Royalpass & UC clock widget.

  • Find accounts on the devices

    Free Royalpass & UC does not recognize or save your account information for any programs. Free Royalpass & UC only detects whether users have a Google account linked with the device which helps us confirm the state of Google Service and provide users with appropriate application download and update methods.

  • Read Google service configuration

    This information is used to acquire an advertising ID. Free Royalpass & UC provides users with better advertising service by using such anonymous ID.

  • Change network connectivity

    This permission is used in Free Royalpass & UC settings and notification toolbar, in order to change/check network connectivity.

  • Connect and disconnect from Wi-Fi

    This permission is used in Free Royalpass & UC settings and notification toolbar in order to connect and disconnect from Wi-Fi.

  • Full network access

    This permission is used to access the device’s network for certain functions including receiving update notifications or accessing app classification labels.

  • View network connections

    This permission is used in Free Royalpass & UC settings and notification toolbar, in order to view network connections.

  • View Wi-Fi connections

    This permission is used in Free Royalpass & UC settings and notification toolbar, in order to view Wi-Fi connections.

  • Access Bluetooth settings

    This permission is used in Free Royalpass & UC settings, in order to turn on and off the Bluetooth.

  • Pair with Bluetooth devices

    This permission is used in Free Royalpass & UC settings, in order to pair with Bluetooth devices.

  • Close other apps

    This permission is used in Free Royalpass & UC Boost in order to turn off the back-end apps and make the phone run faster.

  • Retrieve running apps

    This permission is used in Free Royalpass & UC Boost in order to view the running apps.

  • Run as startup

    Free Royalpass & UC will run as a startup to provide users with a launcher service.

  • Draw over other apps

    This permission allows the Free Royalpass & UC Free Swipe tab to be displayed as a floating window on other apps.

  • Control vibration

    This permission allows Free Royalpass & UC to make the phone vibrate once after users set the device on vibrate, confirming the setting has been turned on.

We use this information to boost your Android phone effectively, clean your device's RAM and make your phone speed up in depth.
